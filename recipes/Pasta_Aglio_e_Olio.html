    Ingredients
  • 1 Pound of Dried Spaghetti Noodles
  • 1/2 cup of Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 8 Cloves of Fresh Chopped Garlic
  • 1 Bunch of Flat Leaf Italian Parsley Chopped
  • 1/4 cup Freshly Grated Parmesean
  • 1/4 teaspoon Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

  1. Start by bringing a large pot of heavely salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the the bite(al dente for those in the know). before draining the pasta reserve 1/4 cup of the cooking water for later. Drain the pasta and set aside.

  2. Combine garlic and olive oil in a cold skillet. Cook over medium heat to slowly toast garlic, about 10 minutes. Reduce heat to medium-low when olive oil begins to bubble. Cook and stir until garlic is golden brown, about another 5 minutes. Remove from heat.
